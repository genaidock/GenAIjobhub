## 1. Vision & Mission Statement

### Vision
> **To become the world's most trusted and specialized AI talent marketplace — the definitive destination where India's AI professionals and global AI employers find their perfect match.**

### Mission

GenAIJobHub.com exists to eliminate the noise of generalist job boards by creating a purpose-built AI ecosystem where every listing, candidate, and opportunity is rigorously AI-relevant.

We empower India's rapidly growing AI workforce — engineers, researchers, prompt engineers, AI product managers, and freelancers — with curated full-time roles, contract projects, and learning pathways that accelerate their careers at the speed of AI advancement.

We serve global AI-first employers and Indian enterprises alike by delivering pre-qualified, AI-literate talent faster, cheaper, and more precisely than any generalist platform can.

5. **Education-to-Employment Gap:** IITs, IIMs, and private universities produce ~120,000 AI/ML graduates annually, but placement infrastructure for AI-specific roles is fragmented and generalist.

## Appendix: Strategic Priorities Summary

### The 3 Non-Negotiable Strategic Bets

1. **AI Curation Quality > Quantity:** Never compromise on listing quality. One spam listing damages trust more than 10 great listings build it. Invest in AI-assisted curation from Day 1.

2. **Community Before Product:** The platform will win on community (newsletter, LinkedIn, podcast, events) before the product is feature-complete. Community creates SEO, brand, and referrals — the three cheapest acquisition channels.

3. **India-First, Globe-Ready:** Build every feature with Indian users and INR in mind first. Only expand to USD pricing or non-India employers after the India market is locked. This focus prevents the "generalist drift" that kills vertical platforms.
